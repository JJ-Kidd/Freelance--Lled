Meet the Team

LLED Construction Ltd. was founded in 1995 by our current Operations Director Mr. Dee Addison with the stated objective to serve the emerging new reactive maintenance marketplace.

Initially the company mainly traded as a strategic partner or sub-contractor to the main national players in this field. During this time the company was twice awarded the title of Sub-contractor of the year by the maintenance arm of Mansell plc.

As the company grew it developed and cultivated its own direct work client base, raising to its current status of delivering direct to the client regional reactive maintenance services whilst still providing strategic partnering to national maintenance service providers. In 2016 LLED construction was wholly acquired by its longstanding client Morris and Spottiswood Ltd. Dee was retained as a director to oversee the running of the business and the ultimate integration into the maintenance arm of our new owners in the near future.

Dee Addison | Operations Director

Dee has over thirty years’ experience working within construction the past twenty seven of which have been in senior management roles. In the early years of his construction career he worked as a bricklayer and thus has the benefit of hands on experience, throughout this career his work has mainly focused on the maintenance and repair of commercial premises. With his detailed knowledge and extensive experience he is able to offer great depth of service to clients advising them on resolutions to current problems and offering plans to prevent future issues arising.
