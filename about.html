Reactive Property Maintenance Services

LLED Construction Ltd is an award winning reactive maintenance service provider to retail, banking, entertainment and commercial businesses.

Key features of our comprehensive Property Maintenance Services:

  • 24/7 response service to all trades work delivered by LLED engineers
  • In house Help Desk manned by LLED employees
  • Fully automated call handling system for logging, tracking, updating and reporting calls with remote client access facility
  • Uniformed fully trained operatives with photographic ID cards, liveried tracked vehicles and mobile 3G contact facility
  • Transparent pre-agreed schedules of rates and cost control procedures
  • Job escalation procedures and key performance indicators (KPI's) as per each customer's requirements
  • Full documentary backup and electronic invoicing provided as required
  • Calls received by phone, fax, email or internet

  • Creation of planned preventative maintenance programmes to suit clients needs
  • Transparent costing on pre agreed maintenance schedules
  • Fully automated call handling system for logging, tracking, updating and reporting calls with remote client access facility
  • Full on line call reporting with remote customer access
  • Regular client review meetings
  • Weekly progress reports provided as required
